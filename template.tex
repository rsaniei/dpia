1.To decide whether a DPIA is required for a specific processing operation.\newline
2.To decide whether a DPIA is complete in terms of the information it should contain. If not, what are the necessary actions to comply with the Regulation (for example, prior consultation with the DPAs)? }                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      \\ \hline
